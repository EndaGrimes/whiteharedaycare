At White Hare Day Care we are passionate about children’s learning and education. We provide a secure, loving and caring environment for all children that come into our care.
We work hard to build strong, respectful relationships with our children and their families from the first moment they step into our centre. We believe that we share the responsibility with parents and the wider community of working in collaboration to educate children to support them in their growth and development.
We encourage a positive self-image where everyone is treated with equal respect. Our staff at White Hare Day Care role model this to our children on a daily basis to help develop sensitivity towards others, multicultural awareness and a positive approach to learning. At White Hare Day Care we do not discriminate against children or their families for reasons such as race, religion, sex or ability.
We value children’s choice, allowing children to learn, grow and flourish at their own pace and ability. At White Hare Day Care we encourage independence and self-help skills in the aim of preparing children not just for school, but for life. “Never help a child with a task at which they feel they can succeed.” – Maria Montessori
