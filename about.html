Our Statement of Purpose

At White Hare Day Care we are passionate about children’s learning and education. We provide a secure, loving and caring environment for all children that come into our care.
At White Hare Day Care we are passionate about children’s learning and education. We provide a secure, loving and caring environment for all children that come into our care.
We work hard to build strong, respectful relationships with our children and their families from the first moment they step into our centre. We believe that we share the responsibility with parents and the wider community of working in collaboration to educate children to support them in their growth and development.
We encourage a positive self-image where everyone is treated with equal respect. Our staff at White Hare Day Care role model this to our children on a daily basis to help develop sensitivity towards others, multicultural awareness and a positive approach to learning. At White Hare Day Care we do not discriminate against children or their families for reasons such as race, religion, sex or ability.
We value children’s choice, allowing children to learn, grow and flourish at their own pace and ability. At White Hare Day Care we encourage independence and self-help skills in the aim of preparing children not just for school, but for life. “Never help a child with a task at which they feel they can succeed.” – Maria Montessori

History

There is a long history of childcare provision associated with the Creggan area, with the White Hare playgroup existence from 1990 until 2005. This facility was a recognised leader in this field for many others that came behind.
With the closure of Creggan P.S. in 2010, Creggan community Association (CCA), following many appeals for childcare assistance, began a process of research and consultation which ndicated a strong need. However following fruitless attempts to acquire the necessary funding , CCA began intensive fundraising which culminated in the renovation of the old Creggan Community Centre.
Today we are proud of our state of the art, purpose built, Children's Daycare Centre which we are even prouder to name White Hare Daycare - Creggan.
We hope that this non profit business will grow in suppost of the community that it serves and will in itself set the template for other groups wishing to provide a similar service.

What We Offer

Our baby room caters for a maximum of 9 babies. This room is self-contained with a changing area and has its own sleep room. The room is set up to encourage exploration and stimulation of our senses. Each baby’s day will be closely monitored with parents and carers updated appropriately.
Our Pre School room will cater up to a maximum of 16 children. It too is self-contained with its own toileting and storage area. This room will also have a structured day with monitored activities and children’s independence evident in all aspects of their day. Our Pre School program will incorporate literacy, numeracy, science and technology, art and physical skills, all through play based learning.
White Hare Day Care has a Toddler Room which can cater for a maximum of 12 children. Again this room is self-contained with it’s own changing/toileting facilities and sleep room. This room will have an activity based day focusing on self-help skills to encourage independence.
All rooms will have the opportunity for outdoor learning and play activities, weather permitting. Educators will carry out planning and observations to ensure that activities meet the needs of individual children.
At White Hare Day Care we will provide all day supervision from 7.30am through to 6pm, Monday to Friday.
During a full day, children will receive one hot meal and two snacks during the day.

What your child needs when starting White Hare Day Care

    In your child’s bag please include –

  • Nappies (if applicable)
  • Nappy cream
  • Baby wipes
  • Soother (if required for sleep, with labelled box for storage).
  • Comforter
  • Drinking Cup
  • Coat - (for outdoor play)
  • Wellie boots - (can be stored at the centre)
  • Hat (either sun hat or woolly hat, weather depending)
  • Multiple changes of clothing
  • Cot sheet and blanket (for nap time) inside a pillow case (for storage)
  • At Whitehare Day Care, we LOVE messy play!!

    • For younger babies who require formula -
  • A tin of formula (to be left at the centre)
  • Bottles – send appropriate number of pre sterilised bottles required each day.

    • Breastfeeding is welcome at White Hare Day Care.
  • If providing breast milk to be given in bottles please speak with a staff member regarding storage and heating requirements.

Meal Times

White Hare Day Care ensures that children are offered a well-balanced and nutritious diet. Our menus are available in each room. The menus are rotated on a three week basis and parents and carers feedback is always welcome.
During the enrolment process at White Hare Day Care, you will be asked about your child/children’s dietary requirements including any allergies. We will cater for these requirements as far as possible.

  • Breakfast is available between 7.30am and 8.30am.
  • A progressive morning snack will be available around 9.45am.
  • Lunch is served around 12pm / 12.30pm.
  • A progressive evening snack is provided around 3pm.

    • Meal times and routines are flexible, with routines being monitored and reviewed if necessary, to meet the needs of the children in each room.
