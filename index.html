Welcome

Welcome to White Hare Daycare Creggan. My name is Catherine Donnelly and I am the Daycare Manager. I wish to welcome you as a new member of our extended family

White Hare Daycare is a community business and a non-profit organisation. At White Hare we are committed to providing high quality professional childcare in a rural community.

White Hare Daycare is currently registered for a combined number of 37 places.

At White Hare Daycare, the welfare of the children is paramount and we will keep parents and carers fully informed throughout their time in our care.

What do I need to do?

If you are interested in our services at White Hare Daycare - Creggan, you should take the following steps:

1. Discuss with the Manager availability of places, days and times.
2. Complete an enrolement form and return to White Hare Daycare with a Â£50 deposit to secure your place. (Which will be taken off your first bill)
3. Sign a contract with White Hare Daycare for your child's place.
4. Begin leaving your child in for short periods of time (agreed with the Manager in advance)
5. Complete a Direct Debit mandate for the payment of your childcare bill.
6. Find out if you are eligible for financial support such as Tax Free Childcare.
